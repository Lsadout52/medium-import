Getting Started with Amazon Bedrock

Amazon Bedrock is a fully managed service that allows you to build and scale generative AI applications using foundation models (FMs) from top providers via a single API. With no need to manage infrastructure, Bedrock lets developers integrate powerful models into their apps securely and efficiently.

What Makes Bedrock Unique?

Unlike traditional ML services where you train or deploy your own models, Bedrock gives access to pre-trained foundation models from providers like Anthropic (Claude), AI21 Labs, Stability AI, Cohere, Meta, and Amazon Titan. You can use these models for text generation, summarization, image creation, chatbots, and more‚Äîall without provisioning servers.
